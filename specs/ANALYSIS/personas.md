    *   Income Level: Fixed income (pension, social security)
*   **Technical Proficiency**: Novice (uses email, basic web browsing, but easily confused by complex interfaces or too much information). Prefers clear, simple navigation.
*   **Primary Goals**:
    1.  Resolve lingering collection accounts and clean up credit after a period of identity theft.

*   **Key Quote**: "I just want to make sure I'm not being taken advantage of, especially after what happened with my identity. I need someone honest and patient to help me sort this out without any hidden fees."
*   **Success Scenario**: Eleanor is referred to Top Tier by a trusted friend.
