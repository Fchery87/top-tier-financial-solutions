## 1. System Overview

The Top Tier Financial Solutions website will be a modern, responsive marketing platform designed for high trust, legal compliance, and lead generation. It leverages a server-rendered frontend framework for performance and SEO, with minimal backend services primarily for form submissions and analytics. Third-party integrations will handle scheduling and web analytics.
